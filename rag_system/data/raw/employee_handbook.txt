Employee Handbook

Welcome to the Company!

Onboarding

New employees undergo a two-week onboarding program that covers company culture, tools and systems, team introductions, and role-specific training. Each new hire is assigned a buddy for their first month.

Benefits

Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of employment. The company offers dental, vision, and life insurance options. A 401(k) retirement plan with up to 4% company matching is available after 90 days of employment.

Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year, in January and July. Reviews assess goal achievement, collaboration, and professional development. A self-assessment must be submitted one week before the review meeting.

Training and Development

Employees have access to an annual training budget of $2,000 for conferences, courses, and certifications. Approval from the manager is required for any training expenditure.

IT Policies

Company-issued laptops must be returned upon termination. Software installations require IT approval. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
